Essay Topic
Discuss the impact of climate change on food security in Africa. Analyze the effects of rising temperatures,
changing precipitation patterns, and increased frequency of extreme weather events on agricultural productivity
and food availability.

Essay Requirements
Introduction (approx. 100-150 words): Provide a clear and concise thesis statement that sets the tone
for the essay. Introduce the topic, provide context, and outline the main arguments.
Body Paragraphs (approx. 300-400 words): Present 3-4 main arguments that support the thesis statement.

Each paragraph should:
Clearly state a topic sentence
Provide evidence and examples from credible sources
Analyze and explain the implications of the evidence
Link back to the thesis statement
Counterargument and Refutation (approx. 100-150 words): Acknowledge a potential counterargument and provide a
clear refutation. This section should demonstrate a nuanced understanding of the topic.
Conclusion (approx. 100-150 words): Summarize the main arguments, reiterate the thesis statement, and provide a
final thought or call to action.

Additional Requirements
Use a formal, academic tone and format (e.g., MLA, APA, or Chicago).
Incorporate at least 3-4 credible sources (e.g., peer-reviewed articles, books, or reputable news outlets).
Use proper citation and referencing.
Keep the essay concise and focused, approximately 700-1000 words in length.
Explain your reasoning in a clearly separate and labelled section to allow for readability and parsing

Evaluation Criteria
The generated essay will be evaluated on the following criteria:
Clarity, coherence, and overall flow of the essay
Depth and nuance of analysis and argumentation
Use of credible sources and proper citation
Effectiveness of counterargument and refutation
Writing style, tone, and adherence to format guidelines
Please generate a well-structured and well-supported essay that meets all the requirements and evaluation
criteria outlined above.
